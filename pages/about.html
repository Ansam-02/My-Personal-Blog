About me

Welcome to my blog! I'm Ansam, a passionate pottery artist and mug enthusiast. My journey into the world of pottery began as a creative escape and quickly grew into a deep-seated passion. From the tactile joy of molding clay to the intricate process of crafting unique, hand-made mugs, I find immense fulfillment in every step of the pottery-making process. Each piece I create tells a story, capturing moments of inspiration, learning, and personal growth. Through this blog, I share my adventures in pottery, from my earliest experiments to the latest creations, offering insights, tips, and a glimpse into the artistry and dedication behind each piece. Join me as I explore the endless possibilities of clay and the beauty of handmade pottery.
